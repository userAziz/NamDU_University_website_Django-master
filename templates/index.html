Namangan State University

Namangan State University (NamSU) was established in 1942 as a pedagogical institute. It is in Namangan province which has almost 2.8 mln population with 7900 km2 area locatedin eastern part on Uzbekistan, considered one of the strategically important area. Nowadays NamSU has over 15000 students in bachelor and master degree programs.
